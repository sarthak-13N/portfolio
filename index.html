About Me

Hello, I'm Sarthak!

I'm Sarthak Nimble, a dedicated and enthusiastic Software Developer with a strong foundation in full-stack development, embedded systems, and DevOps. My journey in tech is driven by a passion for building scalable and impactful software solutions.

I specialize in frontend technologies like React.js and Angular, and backend frameworks such as Spring Boot and Node.js, with database expertise in MySQL and PostgreSQL. I also bring experience in embedded programming, system optimization, and cloud platforms like AWS.

Over the years, I've worked on diverse projects — from building smart agriculture systems like CropDoc to developing enterprise platforms such as QuantumCorp and Absensync. My work reflects a blend of innovation and problem-solving.
